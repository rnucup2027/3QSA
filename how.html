How to Play Tokyo Toss Tempest

Control the basket

Use your cursor or finger to move the basket to accurately catch the parcels.

Avoid dropping the food

Don't let the sushi and boba touch the floor! The customers won't be able to consume it if they do.

The Only Exception

You can let the potion touch the floor. Your score will be devastated if you catch it.

How to Stop the Man from Driving

You can't. It's his life's destiny.

Keep in Mind....

Don't forget to enjoy the game and live the moment! Life is short.
