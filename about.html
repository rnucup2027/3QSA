About the Game

The objective of the game is for the player to utilize the basket to gather all of the safe food after the delivery man abruptly threw all of his packages into the air. Nobody knows why the delivery person is carrying a dangerous potion that, if consumed, could kill the recipient.

Our group decided to pursue this game as a recreation of the fruit catcher games that we found on YouTube.

How to Ace Tokyo Toss Tempest

Keep Track of the Basket

The basket stays at the bottom of the page and follows your cursor. Make sure your aim is alright.

Boba = 1 point; Sushi = 3 points

Aim to catch sushi since it incurs more points!

Beware of the Mysterious Potion

You don't have to catch everything that falls. Think of the consequences that occur if you capture something that you shouldn't.

Don't Let the Parcels Fall

Important things must not hit the ground and get hurt. Save them while you can!

Keep in Mind....

Don't forget to enjoy the game and live the moment! Life is short.
